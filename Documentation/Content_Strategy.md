## Thumbnail & Title Formulas

### High-CTR Title Patterns

- **"Can AI [SKILL]?"** - Can AI Learn to Parry? Can AI Beat Malenia?
- **"AI vs [FAMOUS THING]"** - AI vs Dark Souls, AI vs Every Boss
- **"[NUMBER] Hours of [THING]"** - 30 Hours of AI Training, 50,000 Hours vs 1 Hour
- **"Why AI Can't [THING]"** - Why AI Can Play DS But Not Pokemon
- **"Before vs After"** - Always works for training content

### Thumbnail Elements

- Game screenshot with AI overlay/indicator
- Boss health bar (for boss fight videos)
- Split screen (before/after, AI vs Human)
- NVIDIA/AI branding subtle but visible
- Shocked/reacting face (optional, works for fails)
